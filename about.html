   Trajectory

Born with a love for mathematics, inherited from my dad. Some of my favorite childhood memories with him are waking up at 4am to work on math problems.

I was about 10 years old, when I got inspired by my older cousin to pursue Engineering. So after completing high school, I ended up pursuing Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

I wanted to learn more about how things work, so I pursued a Master's degree in Embedded Electrical and Computer Systems with a focus on VLSI Design.

After my Master's, I joined Intel Corporation and have worked as a Product Development Engineer, where I developed semiconductor products with a wide breadth of experience across circuit design, validation, modelling, simulations, silicon debug, failure analysis, defining the post silicon test plans, data analysis and High Volume Manufacturing. Gained hands on experience with Multimeters, Oscilloscope, Logic Analyzer, Wafer prober and Automated Test Equipment.

During this time, test automation and data analytics became an integral part of my work. I used data to make informed decisions, optimize processes, and improve product performance. This experience sparked my interest in AI, ML and thier applications in optimizing electrical engineering workflows.

In 2024, I decided to formalize my AI and ML skills and enrolled in intensive training programs and hands on project work. I am now looking forward to meaningfully contribute to the next generation of AI driven semiconductor development.
